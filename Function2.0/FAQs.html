Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What types of coffee do you offer?

‎We offer a variety of coffee drinks, including Espresso, Americano, Latte, Mocha, and beverages. We also have non-coffee options such as tea, strawberry milk, and chocolate.

Can I order coffee online?

‎Yes! You can place your order through our website. We offer pickup order.

What are your operating hours?

We are open every day from 12:00 PM to 12:00 AM.

‎Do you serve pastries or snacks?

Yes, we offer some Nibblers like French Fries and Quesadillas, also have Desserts like Belgian Waffles and Croffles with different toppings.

Do you offer Wi-Fi?

Yes, we do! Feel free to connect while you enjoy your time here.
