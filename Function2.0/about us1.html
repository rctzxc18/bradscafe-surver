About Us

The business officially started in January 2024 owned by Mr. Manuel Vasquez. It was located at 969 Avenue Barangay 314, Santa Cruz, Manila, Mr. Vasquez shared that the store is inspired by the thought of people sharing a meal with a special someone just like an everyday meal that is shared at home. His passion for creating memorable dining experiences led him to open the café offering delicious coffee and food. Brad’s Kitchen and Café opens from 12 PM to 12AM, from Monday to Sunday.
